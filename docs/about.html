About FairCloudCrafter

Salesforce apps,
fairly priced.

We're two senior Salesforce engineers building the tools the big consultancies overcharge for. No investors. No middlemen. No markup. Just the apps you wish existed, priced like a developer would price them.

Why we exist

Salesforce is the most powerful business platform on earth. The apps built on top of it are some of the most expensive software in the industry. That gap is unfair to everyone except the incumbents.

Conga charges $35/user/month. Titan charges $80. Drawloop sells implementation services on top. Meanwhile, the actual code that powers their products would be a weekend project for a senior engineer who knows the platform.

FairCloudCrafter is the answer: senior Salesforce engineers, building the apps the big consultancies overcharge for, priced at what they actually cost to make and maintain. Fair pricing. Native LWC. No agents, no Heroku, no DPA gymnastics.

How we build

Four principles. No exceptions.

⚖️

Fair pricing

If we can build it for X, we charge close to X plus a fair margin. No private-equity playbook, no "anchor on enterprise pricing." We can afford this because we don't have investors to pay back.

🔧

Native by default

Every product runs inside Salesforce, on Salesforce, with Salesforce APIs. No external services. No agents on Heroku. No data egress that requires a procurement battle.

🛠️

Crafted, not assembled

We're a small team that ships production-quality code. We won't grow headcount we don't need. We won't ship features that don't earn their place.

📖

Transparent by default

Public roadmap. Public market analysis. Open-source free tiers. Our customers and prospects deserve to see how the sausage is made.
